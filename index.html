About Me

Hello! My name is John Tran and I am 25 years of age from Southern California. However, I was not born in California. I was born on the east coast in one of the oldest cities in America, Boston, Massachusetts. I moved to Souther California when I was 12 years old and have lived here ever since. I attended the University of California, Irvine and I am the graduating class of 2015 as a Public Health Sciences major. At the time I was pursueing a career in nursing and possibly have gone to nursing school. Currently this plan has changed.

I have decided that I want to take a coding bootcamp to successfull transition into the technology field. I have always been tech savy and enjoyed the use of technology since I was a kid. This decision took a while for me to make considering I graduated over 2 years ago to date. I hope to find my passion in coding and hopefully a new career in the process.
